Tinysat Viz: Example

This demonstration visualizes how the search (and clause learning) process of a basic CDCL SAT solving algorithm works.

Enter a CNF formula in DIMACS format.
Select the demonstration speed, and click "Run!" to start.

When the demonstration runs, the current partial truth assignment is depicted, as well as the stack of made branching decisions. Whenever the search runs into a conflict, the conflict graph is depicted. Also, various solver events are listed in the log.
